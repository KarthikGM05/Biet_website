Forum Activities

COMPUTER SCIENCE FORUM (UniCS)

UniCS provides a platform for the students to exhibit their talents and skills. UniCS conducts variuos technical and non-technical events. UniCS conducts several short term courses and technical talks by inviting experts from industry and academia, organize industry and research centre visits and conduct personality development programs which may help in the placement.

The following events are conducted regularly by UniCS:

Sl.No. Event Event Descrption
1. TCS TechBytes IT quiz competition which provides a better understanding of IT as Technology and is open to all stream students. Preliminary round will be held at Institute level.
2. C-Quest Coding contest which is open to all streams. Students from various institutes participate
3. BPL Cricket Tournament Inter-Departmental cricket tournament.
4. Mock Interviews Skype mock interview are organized to give a mirroring of actual job interview and to bring confidence in students.
5. Certification courses in association with Industry Certification course in association with IT Industry are planned and executed. (UiPath RPA, VMware Badge Day, Salesforce )
6. Seminars/Workshops Conducted and delivered by Industry Resource persons.
7. Invited Talks/ Guest Lectures Technical talks (Digital Marketing, StartUp World)
8. Hackthons/Learnthons Organized by SmartBridge in association with IBM and ICT Academy.
9. In-House Project Exhibition Progyan-1.0
10. Health Awareness Program To address the issues related to Oral health care.
11. Outreach Activities Visiting rural schools frequently to reduce technology barriers. (Awareness to ICT and online Govt. schemes and services)
