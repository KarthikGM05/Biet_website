The Hostel is founded in 1979 to accommodate the boys, who come from different parts of the world. The hostel now has three blocks accommodating about 608 boarders. The rooms are well furnished and the surroundings of hostel is very green and pleasant, which encourages the students to study during late nights. The students who stayed in the hostel have secured 18 university ranks right from the inception of the hostel. The hostel is having two dinning halls of North & South Indian to cater the needs of the boarders for their taste.


Hostel is known for its discipline and well managed by dedicated faculty & staff members The Hostel area is spacious as much as 4.5 acres., built up area it self being 12,877.09 Sq. feet with 200 Rooms accommodating 608 students. Hostel supports the Institute to arrange many programme.

    The Hostel was founded in 2008 to accommodate girl students, who come from different parts of the world. The hostel now has one block with 4 Floors, accommodating about 270 boarders. The rooms are well furnished and the surroundings of hostel is very green and pleasant, which encourages the students to study. The hostel is having two dinning halls to cater to the needs of the boarders for their varied taste. Hostel is known for its discipline and well managed by dedicated faculty & staff members. The hostel is spacious with a built up area of 5029 Sq. mts with 84 Rooms accommodating 252 students.


    The Chairman of “AMRUTHA”, Dr.S.S.D.J Ladies Hostel is Sri.A.C.Jayanna, Treasurer, BEA, Dvg . The warden of the hostel is Prof.Ramesh.S.Manoli. The hostel is well managed by a team of experienced office staff.North Indian & south Indian dishes are prepared in the hostel mess. The hostel mess is managed by the hostel administration itself. Ragging is totally prohibited in the hostel.


    The hostel has indoor shuttle court, and an indoor sports hall which is used for table tennis, chess, carom board and other indoor games.Sports competitions are held regularly to encourage the students to participate in non-curricular activities.


    For the benefit of the boarders a stationery shop is established in the hostel premises. A walking track is also laid all around the hostel. All the national festivals are celebrated in the hostel. The hostel is surrounded by a landscaped garden.


    The boarders actively participate in the national functions, freshers’day and fare well function which are held regularly every year.
