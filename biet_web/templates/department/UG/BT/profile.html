Vision

To impart quality education for translating knowledge to the widest domains and create knowledge assets. To contribute in creating a pool of young engineer to meet the demands of industry and society, through excellence in technical education and research, to remain a source of pride to the nation.



Mission

To be an educational centre of international excellence in technical learning and innovation to help every learner to be successful. To emphasize its foundational heritage in technical education, facilitate interdisciplinary learning, foster research and produce graduates who are technologically astute, think critically and demonstrate proficiency. >
