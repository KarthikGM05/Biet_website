Laboratory Facilities

The Laboratory of Department of physics are utilized by M.Tech., Dental, Medical college students for their projects to conduct.


Major Equipments

  • He-Ne Gas Laser of 5mW.

  • Four Probe set up for measurement of conductivity of material.

  • Ultrasonic interferometer to measure Ultrasonic wave velocity and allied parameters.

  • Kits of Transistor characteristics, LCR resonance setup with AFO.

  • Cathode Ray Oscilloscopes.

  • B-H Curve setup for measuring magnetic fields.
