Vision

Our Vision is to working closely with other faculties and technocrats to see excellence with diffuse departmental boundaries, to interact among different disciplines to make our students continually increase their intellect and critical thinking capabilities through academic challenges, as capable users of technology.



Mission

Our Mission is to create and maintain an interdisciplinary, well renowned, research environment that generates fundamental and technical knowledge about physical phenomena and produces highly skilled technocrats at all levels so as to share this knowledge in empowering, appropriate and relevant ways with all members of society through classroom teaching, training and outreach .
