Forum Activities

  • As an annual feature Textile forum organizes two days national level students Technical Symposium. Students from all over the country participate in this symposium from textile & fashion technology institutes.

  • Special Lecture programme are organized every year for the students. Experts from industry & R& D will deliver the lectures on exclusive topics.

  • Forum organizes “Personality development programmes every year to final & pre-final year students jointly with placement cell of our institute.

  • Entrepreneurship development programmes are organized every year in association with textile & handloom sectors.

  • In addition Forum organizes TEX Quiz, Sports, Rangoli & Cultural activates to the students.
