One of the premier Engineering Departments of the Institute, the Department of Civil Engineering was established in the year 1979. The department offers B.E, M Tech in Environmental Engineering. It is proposed to introduce M Tech programme in Structural Engineering. The Department actively promotes curriculum development by updating existing courses, developing new courses and preparing resource material for teaching. The Department has a number of ongoing research projects funded by AICTE/ KSTSC/VTU and sponsored research schemes from public and private sector organizations. It undertakes industrial consultancy work as a part of its interaction with industry and also organizes seminars / Symposia/Conferences for professional interaction. The department contributes to the overall progress of civil engineering and Environmental Engineering fields. Environmental Engineering laboratory and Civil Engineering are equipped with alb analysis latest instruments for analysis Environmental parameters, material testing and designing. The department has a well established computer and audio visual center.
