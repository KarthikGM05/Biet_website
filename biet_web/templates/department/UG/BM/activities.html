Activities

FORUM ACTIVITIES

Industry Institute Interaction and Research activity:
  • Institute has an MOU with Applied Cognition Systems Pvt. Ltd. (APCOG Systems), Bangalore - a company dealing with Biomedical Instrumentation and healthcare product development. R&D lab has been established in the Department of Biomedical Engineering to provide Technical Training to meet industry ambience and R&D needs. Project review meetings by APCOG Technical team are held every month to guide project batches.
  • Two batches of final year students from Biomedical Engineering have successfully completed project work in R&D lab of the Department during the academic year 2013 - 14.
  • Eight batches of final year students from Biomedical Engineering, Electronics & Instrumentation and Electronics & Communication Engineering have successfully completed project work in R&D lab of the Department during the academic year 2014 - 15.
  • BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING FORUM: The Biomedical Engineering forum provides a platform for the students to exhibit their talents in the co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Forums conducts technical talks by inviting experts from industry and hospitals, organize hospital visits and conduct personality development programs which may help in the placement.
  • BM SHUTTLE BADMINTON CUP (BM CUP): The department under the banner of Biomedical Engineering forum organize shuttle badminton tournament BM CUP every year. This is one of the most popular sport events for the students since from 2006.
  • BME EXPLORE-2012: Department has organized 5th National Level Students Symposium "BME EXPLORE-2012" on 26th April 2012. The objective of this Symposium was to provide a common platform to UG & PG Students of Biomedical Engineering, Medical Electronics, Biotechnology, Instrumentation Technology, MBBS, BDS and related branches from all over India to come under one umbrella in this symposium to discuss the recent trends in Biomedical Engineering and Medical field. To provide a platform for students to actively participate in the various technical events and provide ample scope to exhibit their talent, which will help in their career development. There was an overwhelming response from student community for BME EXPLORE-2012 and the program was a grand success. Papers Received 70, papers selected 60, papers presented 58, Colleges participated 18, Total number of participants 160.
