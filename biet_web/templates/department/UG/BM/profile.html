Vision

To become a centre of excellence by promoting Biomedical Engineering Education and Research of high quality to make the Department a top choice for undergraduate and postgraduate education.



Mission

To prepare skilled graduates with social and ethical values to be immediately productive and adapt for rapidly changing technology, and to create and apply knowledge at the interface of Life Sciences and Engineering for the benefit of society and for the improvement of human health.
