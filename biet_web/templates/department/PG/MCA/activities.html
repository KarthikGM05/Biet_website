MCA FORUM

FORUM ACTIVITIES: MCA Forum started in the year 2008 with the aim of providing a platform for the MCA students to become Industry-ready along with gaining the knowledge that helps them to become confident, ready to accept challenges and be competent enough to face the outside world. In this regard Forum is striving very hard by conducting department, Inter department and Intern colleges level activities like programming contests, quiz, group discussion, workshop and seminar etc. The following Activities have been conducted since 2008..
