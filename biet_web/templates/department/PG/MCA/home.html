To cater to the needs of students, Bapuji Institute of Engineering & Technology (BIET) has started Master of Computer Applications (MCA) course with very good infrastructure. MCA is a Post Graduate program of three years course spanning over six semesters, last semester of which is exclusively reserved for project work. This MCA degree meets the current demand and the needs of the software Industries. Our MCA department is well equipped with qualified and experienced staff, computer laboratories and Internet facilities. All these computers are inter-connected structured cables. Optical fiber is used as backbone for campus computer networking. Software includes operating systems like Windows 2003, UnixWare and LINUX. For developmental work, compilers for various programming languages are available on all the operating systems. For rapid development purpose Visual tools are available on different platforms. Students can make use of these facilities to enrich their working knowledge on computer domain. Central library has good number of text books, National and International magazines/journals for referring as well as borrowing exclusively for MCA students. Online National and International journals are subscribed through INDEST-AICTE (Indian National Digital Library in Engineering Science and Technology). A Digital library facility is available for both faculty members and students to refer online journals, which are around 200 numbers. Staff and students can use the Internet facility during college working hours for their academic works. Separate edu-satellite facility with videoconference is available for the students in distance learning programs. So far nearly 600 Students graduated in MCA from this institute are well placed in various National and Multinational companies.
