Principal's Message

" Wisdom is Teacher "

" Work is worship - Guru Basavanna Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man - Swami Vivekananda "

Bapuji Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET), strongly believes in these quotes and puts sincere efforts to impart knowledge to the students, so that student themselves can be their own teachers in future. BIET also tries hard to develop work culture and bring out the perfection that every student will have inside.

Vision of BIET always looks forward to be the best center of excellence globally, so that the young mindsets can fit into the international requirements and become valuable assets to the society. The Institute tries to achieve this vision through its world class teaching methodology of outcome based education which empowers students to be successful in their chosen field with positive approach, leadership qualities and ethical values.

Bapuji Educational Association, Davangere, has put up more than 50 years of valuable service in the field of education and came out as a one of the best Institute in the central part of Karnataka. It is having versatility of education with around 40 different educational institutions as branches. With constant support from management lead by Dr. S. Shivashankarappa, Hon.Secretary and Sri S. S. Mallikarjuna, Hon. Joint Secretary, BIET has grown to be the crown in the field of Engineering education with its vast group of alumni spread over the entire globe serving successfully in various fields of engineering.

My dear aspirants of Engineering Education, I welcome you to this 70 acre green campus with number of building clusters, housing various state-of-art laboratories and library of international standards. BIET feels special for its knowledgeable, qualified and committed staff, with vast collection of books, E-library, unique with online classes through Impartus Lecture Capture system, and an efficient placement cell, modern research facilities, excellent industry-institute interaction, comfortable and pleasant hostel accommodation, sports and physical exercise facilities, NSS and NCC groups, adequate health care facilities, continuous housekeeping, round-the-clock security and care taking student centric environment . On behalf BIET family of around 4000 members, I once again welcome you to join hands with us to build a nation of young minds.
